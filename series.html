Space Grit

Half-human half-alien, the Bronsons have created a family from the destruction of a long-ago, failed supersoldier experiment. Dubbed the Scoriah, they're ignored by the colonial power, Geonate...until the first Scoriah takes a mate. With the possibility that the Scoriah might have progeny, the Geonate are no longer content to let them exist in obscurity. Now the Scoriah fight. To exist. And love.

The SPACE GRIT series starts with the novel Black Ice Heart. Each story in the series is standalone but they do build on one another. Reading in order is recommended but not necessary.

Cinder Mated

Para-talents blend into humanity but not without help. The Cinders keep watch and maintain balance of the elemental powers at their disposal. But a rogue group challenges their authority. The only way to face the growing dangers is to give in to desire and join talents with their mates. Stronger together, the Cinders seek their true partners...and claim them.

Demons of Florida

The world changed forever when a rift in the universe allowed demons to pour into Florida every summer solstice. They brought hellions. They brought destruction and greed. But demons had already been here, living within the human world along with ...others. Wolf-shifters and half-demons. Now the world knows they're here and everything is about to change. A handful of lovers face down the coming chaos. Keeping the peace has never been so hot.
