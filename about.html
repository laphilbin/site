L. A. PHILBIN

also writing as ella drake

science fiction, paranormal, and fantasy romance

As Ella Drake, I am primarily a self-published author as well as a published author with Carina Press. My first published work was Scent of Cin, a novella released May 29th, 2009 by a now-closed Cobblestone Press. This was followed by releases from other small to mid-sized publishers: Ellora's Cave, Liquid Silver Books, Lyrical Press, and Samhain Publishing. When these publishers closed, they returned my rights, and I have self-published these works.

As LA Philbin, an upcoming Science Fiction Romance series is in the works. I hope you enjoy my work. Thank you for reading!
