L. A. PHILBIN

also writing as ella drake

science fiction, paranormal, and fantasy romance

On May 29th, 2009, my first title, Scent of Cin by Ella Drake was published by a now-closed Cobblestone Press. This was followed by releases from other small to mid-sized publishers: Ellora's Cave, Liquid Silver Books, Lyrical Press, and Samhain Publishing. These publishers closed and I have since self-published these titles. As Ella Drake, I continue to have ebooks and audio books published with Carina Press.

As LA Philbin, an upcoming Science Fiction Romance series is in the works. I hope you enjoy my work. Thank you for reading!
